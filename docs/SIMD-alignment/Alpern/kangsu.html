His research interests include high performance and parallel computation and he is supported by a National Physical Science Consortium Fellowship. Kang Su graduated in June 1994 from the California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo with a B.S. in Computer Science. His other activities include weightlifting and food tasting.
